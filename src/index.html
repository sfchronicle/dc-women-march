Portraits in the power of pink

<%= t.include("partials/_social.html") %>
Jan. 30, 2017
Photos by Gabrielle Lurie

Before the Women’s March in Washington, D.C. on Jan. 21, I climbed up into a tree and saw the hundreds of thousands of people prepared to march. After a few minutes, I realized I wanted to hear from individuals. I approached the California group and asked to take portraits and get their perspectives and reasons for being there. I flipped over the back of a poster and used the white side as my backdrop. I got a variety of responses, and it was wonderful to see California residents representing themselves at a powerful march.
